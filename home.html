About

Alex comes from Beautiful British Columbia's Peace River Country. He holds a piano focused Bachelor of Music degree from the University of Alberta and studied piano and vocal performance at the prestigious Grande Prairie Regional College. He has vast ensemble experience performing on tenor/alto saxophone, flute, and as a chorist.

Alex's thorough academic study of piano and musicology results in an evidential approach to piano performance practice and instruction. Two of these elements are:

  • Applying improvisation and tasteful stylism to Baroque keyboard technique.

  • Academic solutions to tempi issues in Beethoven's sonatas and other works.
