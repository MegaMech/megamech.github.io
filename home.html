About

Alex holds a pending Bachelor of Music in piano degree from the University of Alberta.
He has many years of ensemble experience with tenor/alto saxophone, flute, and choir. Alex's thorough academic study of piano musicology results in an evidential approach to piano performance practice.
Applying improvisation to Baroque keyboard technique generates stylistically unique performances. An element often ignored.
He studied the Maelzel Metronome and its related issues and can substantiate several solutions to choosing tempi in Beethoven's piano sonatas.
