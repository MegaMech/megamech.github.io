About

Alex holds a pending Bachelor of Music in piano degree from the University of Alberta.
He has many years of ensemble experience with tenor/alto saxophone, flute, and choir.

Alex's thorough academic study of piano musicology results in an evidential approach to piano performance practice and instruction. Two of these elements are:

  • Applying improvisation to Baroque keyboard technique.

  • Academic solutions to tempi issues in Beethoven's sonatas and other works.

Strive for accuracy, stylism, uniquity.
