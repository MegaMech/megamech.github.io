Canadian pianist and composer Alex Windsor holds a pending Bachelor of Music in piano degree from the University of Alberta. Alex has many years of ensemble experience with tenor/alto saxophone, flute, and in choirs.

Alex's thorough academic study of piano musicology results in an evidential approach to piano performance practice.
Applying improvisation to Baroque keyboard technique generates stylistically unique performances. An element often ignored.
He studied the Maelzel Metronome and its related issues and can substantiate several solutions to choosing tempi in Beethoven's piano sonatas.
Alex thoughtfully constructs information so students can translate words to muscle movements in an easy manner.
